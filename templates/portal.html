What is Rephi?

Rephi is an interactive dashboard used to compare a client's current mortgage to a refinanced loan. The dashboard contains various charts showing clients how much money they will save by refinancing, and exactly when they will breakeven from lender fees.

How to use Rephi

Rephi is designed to be sent to your clients using a unique web address provided to you after subscribing. A free version is also available at rephi-dashboard.com

  1. Signup and subscribe
  2. Customize your dashboard
  3. Send your unique URL to the client

Why use Rephi?

A 2014 study in the Journal of Financial Economics [1] found that nearly 20% of borrowers could have saved substantially when interest rates fell but failed to do so largely due to:

  1. Information barriers relating to refinancing costs,
  2. Procrastination, and
  3. Difficulty understanding the complexity of the refinancing process

Rephi can help address these issues by giving borrowers the information they need to be confident in the decision to refinance. Each graph is presented in a simple and digestible format and includes a detailed description ensuring your clients know exactly how to interpret the information in front of them. Your custom URL is also easily emailed to your clients to remind them of the financial benefits of refinancing. Let Rephi educate your clients on the loan while you focus on following leads.

[1] Keys, Benjamin J., Devin G. Pope, and Jaren C. Pope. "Failure to refinance." Journal of Financial Economics 122.3 (2016): 482-499.
